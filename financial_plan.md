# Financial Plan

## Revenue Streams

Primary:

- Annual enterprise licenses

Secondary:

- Deployment and setup fees
- Custom fine-tuning and integrations
- Consulting and training

## Target Metrics

- Target ARR: ~CHF 500,000
- Customers: 10–15
- Average ARR per customer: CHF 35k–50k

## Cost Structure

Major costs:

- Founder time
- Development and test infrastructure
- Legal and accounting
- Insurance
- Occasional external contractors

Low or absent costs:

- No hyperscale cloud inference
- No large permanent team
- No heavy marketing spend

## Financial Stability Strategy

- Prioritize recurring revenue
- Prefer multi-year contracts
- Avoid dependence on a single customer
- Maintain cash buffer

## Banking Perspective

- Stable ARR
- Low volatility
- Predictable renewals
- Suitable basis for long-term personal financial planning
