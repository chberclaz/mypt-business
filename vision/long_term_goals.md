# Long-Term Goals

## Personal goals

- Build a calm, sustainable business as a solo founder (or very small team)
- Maintain deep technical involvement (engineering-first)
- Avoid venture capital and hypergrowth pressure
- Achieve stable, predictable income suitable for Switzerland
- Enable long-term financial stability, including the ability to purchase a house
- Avoid burnout and excessive operational stress

## Business goals

- Position MyPT as a **high-trust, high-quality, on-prem AI system**
- Serve a small number of serious enterprise customers
- Focus on long-term contracts and low churn
- Prioritize reliability, control, and security over hype

## Explicit non-goals

- Mass-market SaaS
- Consumer-facing product
- Ad-driven or usage-based micropricing
- “Growth at all costs”
- Becoming a hyperscaler or model provider

## Time horizon

This business is designed to be viable and relevant over **5–10 years**, not optimized for short-term exits.
