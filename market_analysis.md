# Market Analysis

## Target Market

MyPT targets organizations that:

- handle sensitive or regulated data
- operate on-prem or in restricted environments
- require full control over AI systems

## Primary Industries

- Cybersecurity and penetration testing
- Banking and financial services
- Manufacturing and industrial engineering
- Medical, pharmaceutical, and biotech
- Government and defense-adjacent organizations
- Research institutions with proprietary IP

## Market Drivers

- Increasing regulation around data privacy (GDPR, sector-specific rules)
- Internal bans or restrictions on cloud-based AI tools
- Rising cost and energy footprint of hyperscale AI
- Growing need to leverage internal knowledge bases

## Competitive Landscape

### Common Alternatives

- Cloud LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- Running open-source LLMs without system integration
- Large AI consulting projects
- Internally developed AI platforms

### MyPT Differentiation

- Complete system, not just a model
- Agentic tool-based reasoning
- Fully offline / on-prem operation
- Predictable cost structure
- Minimal operational overhead for customers

## Positioning

MyPT is positioned as:

> A high-trust, low-footprint AI platform  
> for organizations that value control, privacy, and reliability over scale.
