# Pricing Strategy

## Pricing philosophy

- Enterprise value-based pricing
- High trust, low volume
- Focus on recurring revenue (ARR)
- Avoid per-token or usage-based pricing

## Core pricing (CHF)

### Annual License

- CHF 30,000 – 60,000 per year
- Includes:
  - MyPT platform
  - Updates
  - Basic support

### Initial Deployment / Setup (one-time)

- CHF 10,000 – 25,000
- Covers:
  - On-prem deployment
  - Document ingestion
  - Index tuning
  - Security review
  - Basic user training

### Optional Add-ons

- Custom fine-tuning: CHF 10,000 – 40,000
- Additional departments / workspaces
- Priority support

## Revenue target

- Target ARR: ~CHF 500,000
- Requires:
  - ~10 customers at CHF 50k
  - or ~12–15 customers at CHF 35–40k

## Rationale

- Cheaper than internal build
- Cheaper than AI consulting projects
- Predictable cost for customers
- Predictable income for business
