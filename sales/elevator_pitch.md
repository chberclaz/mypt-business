# Elevator Pitch

## 30-second version

MyPT is a fully local, offline alternative to ChatGPT for organizations that cannot send data to the cloud. It runs entirely on the customer’s own infrastructure, understands internal documents via RAG, and supports agentic tool-based reasoning.

## 60-second version

MyPT is a private, on-prem AI platform designed for environments with sensitive data. It combines a local language model, retrieval-augmented generation over internal documents, and agentic toolcalling — all without external APIs or data leaving the network.

## Key phrases

- “Your data. Your model. Your infrastructure.”
- “Enterprise AI without the cloud.”
- “GPT-like intelligence, fully local.”
- “Agentic AI you can actually trust.”
