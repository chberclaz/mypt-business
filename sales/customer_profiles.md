# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Best-fit customers

- Cybersecurity and pentesting firms
- Banks and financial institutions
- Manufacturing and industrial companies
- Medical, pharma, biotech
- Government and defense-related organizations
- Research institutions with sensitive IP

## Strong buying signals

- ChatGPT or cloud AI is restricted or banned
- Large internal document repositories
- Compliance or audit requirements
- Existing on-prem infrastructure
- Questions like:
  - “Can this run locally?”
  - “Does data ever leave our network?”

## Poor fit customers

- Consumers
- Startups looking for free tools
- Teams without sensitive data
- Organizations expecting zero setup effort
