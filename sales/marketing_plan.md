# Marketing & Sales Plan

This document defines how MyPT is marketed and sold as a solo-founder business.
The goal is quality over volume.

---

## Sales Model

- Direct, relationship-driven enterprise sales
- Founder-led demos and discussions
- Low customer volume, high trust
- Long-term contracts preferred

---

## Target Buyers

- CTOs
- CISOs
- Heads of AI / Data / Knowledge Management
- Technical decision-makers in regulated environments

---

## Primary Channels

- LinkedIn outreach (targeted, non-spammy)
- Direct referrals
- Professional network
- Consulting and systems integration partners
- Industry-specific contacts (security, finance, industrial)

---

## Core Messaging Themes

- “Your data never leaves your infrastructure”
- “Enterprise AI without the cloud”
- “Predictable, controllable AI systems”
- “Agentic AI grounded in your documents”
- “Built for sensitive environments”

---

## Sales Process

1. Initial conversation (fit validation)
2. High-level demo (capabilities, architecture)
3. Pilot or proof-of-concept (optional)
4. Commercial discussion
5. Deployment and onboarding
6. Long-term support relationship

---

## Marketing Philosophy

- No hype-driven claims
- Conservative, honest language
- Emphasis on reliability and control
- Transparency over feature overload
- Avoid mass marketing and lead funnels

---

## What We Explicitly Avoid

- Consumer marketing
- Usage-based micro-pricing
- Aggressive upselling
- Overpromising autonomy or intelligence

---

## Success Metric

A successful marketing effort results in:

- Fewer but better customers
- Low churn
- Long-term trust
- Sustainable founder workload
